ACOLAB
Technical Art and Systems Integration Specialists
ACOLAB (Adelaide Coordinate Laboratories) is a co-op work space and studio, home to some of Adelaide's finest
up-and-coming artists and developers.
Specialising in technical art and systems integration, ACOLAB's Darcy Holmes and Thomas Meakin have worked with Imagine Room, Northern Sound System, Dave Court, Capital Waste Productions, MRLN, Cackling Jackal and others.
